As the children begin to develop trust and confidence, their development are influenced by the experiences they get from their environment hence they should be given consistent nurturance, sensory stimulation, and a good role model.

Mission We are committed in providing the community a well-organized environment that supports and nurtures young children’s intellectual, social, physical, and emotional development. We would like to help children in adjusting to an environment outside the nuclear family. We devote ourselves in providing a good place where parents can safely put their children.

Vision We foresee Clever Minds Development Center to be a part in building a strong community through dedicated training and guidance of children.
